Beginner Trail Highlights + Quick Tips

Start with simple, reliable gear. You do not need the most expensive setup to enjoy the trail. Focus on comfort, weather awareness, and the basics that help you stay safe.

Essentials

Start with the Basics

Bring water, wear supportive shoes, and pack an extra layer. These few basics make a huge difference on short hikes.

Quick Tips

Keep It Light

Check the forecast, tell someone your plans, and only carry what you truly need for the trail and weather conditions.

Trail Reminders

Weather Checklist

  • Check forecast before leaving
  • Bring a light jacket
  • Wear layers for changing temps

Safety Reminders

  • Carry enough water
  • Charge your phone
  • Stay on marked trails
